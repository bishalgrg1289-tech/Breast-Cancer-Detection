<h3>Short discription or idea about project.</h3>
<br>
<p>It is CNN project. It gives one of the three output(Benign, Malignant and Normal).</p>
<ol>
  <li>Benign: Refers to a tumor or growth that is non-cancerous, grows slowly,and does not spread to other parts of the body.<br>
  Usually, it is less harmful and can often be removed surgically.</li>
  <li>Malignant: Refers to a tumor or growth that is cancerous, can grow rapidly, invade nearby tissues, and spread (metastasize) to other parts of the body.<br>
  Malignant tumors are more dangerous and may require aggressive treatment like surgery, chemotherapy, or radiation.</li>
</ol>
